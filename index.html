Dress code

Absolutely informal — whatever you'd normally wear on a Saturday afternoon. Jeans or a fancy hat, really anything goes.

One request: incorporate a rose — in any way you like.

Getting there

We recommended not driving. The Clapham reception is walking distance from Clapham Common and Clapham North (Northern line), or Clapham High Street (Overground).

Kids

Very much welcome. At the ceremony, younger ones on parents' laps or in a row at the front. Plenty of room at the reception, plus an outside space.

Presents

Your presence is present enough — really. But if you wanted to, we had a honeymoon fund with Trailfinders. Reference 5B15TG, surname Lyons or Mesner.
